Project3: Insectispy

InsectiSpy


Firebase
Expo
React Native
Tensorflow
JavaScript
TypeScript

A group project as part of Northcoders. This is an android app to make learning about insects interesting and fun. With this app, children can take pictures of insects they have found, learn their scientific and common names and get a bit of trivia. Finding insects also leads to gaining points, levelling up and unlocking badges.
